Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Overview

Big Things ("the App") is developed by Quyen Ngo. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Location

The App uses Apple Core Location with the "Always" permission to detect when you approach a Big Thing or convict-era heritage site, so that an audio story can play automatically as you drive past. Your location is processed entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to any external server, never logged, never shared, and never sold.

Why "Always" rather than "When in Use": stories are designed to play automatically while you drive — for safety, drivers shouldn't be looking at their phone. Background geofencing requires the "Always" permission. You can revoke or downgrade this permission at any time in iOS Settings; with "While Using" or "Off" the App falls back to manual story playback.

Heard History and Favourites

The App tracks which stories you've heard (so they don't replay too soon) and any stories you mark as favourites. This information is stored locally on your device and is not synced to any external server in this version.

Subscription Data

When you purchase a Big Things subscription, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To play story audio when you approach a Big Thing or convict-era site
  • To suppress recently-heard stories for approximately 7 days
  • To remember your favourites
  • To enforce free-tier daily quotas
  • To process in-app subscriptions through Apple's StoreKit

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Heard-story history
  • Favourites
  • Daily-quota counter (free tier)
  • App settings and preferences

No data is sent to any external server. Big Things operates entirely on-device with no backend, no cloud storage, and no analytics services.

No Account Required

Big Things does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name or email address.

AI-Generated and Curated Content

Stories in Big Things are pre-generated by an AI language model from publicly available sources (Wikipedia under CC BY-SA, UNESCO World Heritage listings, state heritage registers). Stories were reviewed by the developer before being included in the App, but factual errors may still occur. Treat stories as entertainment and as a starting point for your own exploration; do not rely on them as authoritative historical or cultural sources. Sources for each story are listed in the in-app story detail screen.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit: In-app subscriptions are processed by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies.
  • Apple Core Location: Location is processed on-device only. Apple's privacy policy applies.
  • Apple Speech Synthesis (AVSpeechSynthesizer): Story audio is generated on-device using Apple's built-in text-to-speech.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Your location, listening history, and favourites never leave your device.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all your data at any time through the app's Settings or by removing the App from your device.

Children's Privacy

Big Things is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can delete all data through the app's Settings or by removing the App from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
