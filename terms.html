Description of Service

Big Things AU is an iOS app that plays AI-narrated audio stories about Australian roadside landmarks ("Big Things AU") and convict-era heritage sites as you drive past them. Stories are pre-generated, bundled with the App, and triggered automatically by your device's location while the App is running.

Driving Safety

Big Things AU is designed to play audio passively while you drive. Do not interact with the App while driving. Do not look at the screen while operating a vehicle. Audio is intended to be heard, not watched. You are solely responsible for safe driving and complying with all road rules in your jurisdiction.

AI-Generated Content and Accuracy

Stories in Big Things AU are generated by an AI language model from publicly available sources and reviewed by the developer before inclusion. Despite this review, stories may contain factual errors, simplifications, or omissions. The App is offered as entertainment and as a starting point for further exploration, not as an authoritative historical, cultural, or geographic reference. Do not rely on stories for academic, commercial, or legal purposes.

Cultural and Historical Content

Stories about convict-era heritage sites cover historical events including forced labour, colonial expansion, and Indigenous dispossession. Where Aboriginal Country is documented for a site, we name it. Where the historical record reflects harm — to convicts, Aboriginal peoples, women, or children — we acknowledge it factually. We have done our best to tell history truthfully and respectfully. If you find content you believe is inaccurate, harmful, or culturally insensitive, please contact us at the email below; we will review and revise.
